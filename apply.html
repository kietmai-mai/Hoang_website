Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the application process

How long does pre-approval take?

Quick applications are typically reviewed within 2-4 hours during business hours. Full applications with complete documentation can receive pre-approval decisions within 24-48 hours.

What credit score do I need?

We work with borrowers across all credit ranges. Conventional loans typically require 620+, FHA loans can go as low as 580, and we have programs for various credit situations.

How much down payment do I need?

Down payment requirements vary by program: Conventional loans as low as 3%, FHA loans 3.5%, VA loans 0%. We also offer down payment assistance programs for qualified buyers.

Can I apply if I'm self-employed?

Absolutely! We specialize in self-employed lending with bank statement programs, P&L loans, and other flexible income verification options for business owners and entrepreneurs.
